## What is Threat Intelligence?

Threat intelligence is the process of collecting, analysing, and acting on information about current and emerging cyber threats. It helps organisations understand who is targeting them, how they operate, and what they can do to defend against attacks before they happen.

## Key Threat Intelligence Concepts

**IOC — Indicator of Compromise**
Evidence that a system has been compromised such as malicious IP addresses, file hashes, or domain names.

**TTP — Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures**
The behaviour patterns of threat actors. Documented in the MITRE ATT&CK framework.

**Threat Hunting**
Proactively searching for threats that have evaded existing security controls.

**Threat Actor**
An individual or group responsible for carrying out cyber attacks.
